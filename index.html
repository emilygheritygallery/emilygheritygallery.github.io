Emily Gherity Gallery

A curated collection of photographs capturing the life, moments, and story of Emily Gherity. From personal milestones to everyday moments, this gallery invites visitors to explore the journey of her life through images.

Gama’s Best Friend

Gama was an incredibly caring person and particularly cared for those who were vulnerable, making children and animals best friends with Gama.

Did Someone Say Vogue

Gama knew how to enjoy herself, and being able to turn any spare fabric into a catwalk worthy outfit, she always looked stylish while enjoying herself.

Yasss Queens

Gama’s best friend and sister Margie were inseparable, especially when they needed to be separated after an argument where they had been arguing the same side. While you read this there is now doubt they are looking down at you while chatting with a drink in hand.

Radio Ga Gama

Gama and Ga (Bill) had a love that all yearn and few find. They were together for 53 years and in all that time, only spent 4 nights apart (and of course one of those was in jail).

Minnesota Hard

Being a true Minnesotan, Gama wasn’t afraid of a bit of cold, with cold likely only being defined as below -40 in her Minnesotan eyes. And no temperature was too cold to pop outside for a smoke.

Mama Before Gama

Gama raised 6 kids with her devoted partner Ga, which she herself would tell you was her greatest impact on the world.

Gama Balboa

If there was an opportunity for a debate (read argument) Gama would be there ready, and if there wasn’t an opportunity for one, Gama would still be there ready.

Hippie by Nature

Gama lived through the 60s and 70s and boy did it show! There was always an opportunity to showcase her hippie creativity.

Powderhorn 4eva

Gama and Ga spent almost their entire lives together living at Powderhorn park, a place all remember as a haven of love, debate and laughter.

Dancing Queen

Gama wasn’t just a painter, her creativity was displayed on the dance floor too (especially after a few too many beers). And you’ll see her legacy on many a Minnesotan dancefloor where her grandkids continue busting shapes.

Thick as Thieves

The 2 sisters were always laughing and smiling no matter the situation, and this disposition has infected its way into their entire family.

November 72

If you wanted one photo to show Gama’s love of Ga and her family, this one would surely be on high on the list.

Always Center of Attention

The 2 sisters were always laughing and smiling no matter the situation, and this disposition has infected its way into their entire family.

Life In Motion

Raising 6 kids meant having a firm hand and reaping the rewards by enjoying the infinitely many small moments, like this one with youngest child Lea.

Green Sisters

Emily and Margie both loved the outside and were keen, expert gardeners.

Gama Becomes Great-Gama

Gama’s family kept growing and growing, with current count being 6 children, 12 grandchildren and 4 great grandchildren (at time of writing). Gama cared for each with more love than more people receive in their entire lives.
